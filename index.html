Together we build the net we want

Community Networks are a powerful example of how people can build and maintain their own Internet infrastructure — not just to connect devices, but to connect communities.

Unite local efforts for
a global voice for Community Networks

To help these networks grow and thrive, we also need to pay attention to:

  • Supportive legal and policy environments
  • Community governance models that reflect shared values
  • Technical enablers such as community IXPs, local interconnection, and open-source-based services
  • Self-hosted applications that serve real community needs

Together, these elements form a strong and resilient foundation. One that deserves recognition, visibility, and coordination on a global scale.
